
# ⚙️ Planetary Gear Assembly

## Overview

This project is a 3D model and technical drawing of a **planetary gear system** designed in Autodesk Inventor.

The assembly demonstrates how rotational motion is transferred and distributed through a compact gear mechanism commonly used in:
- gearboxes
- automotive transmissions
- mechanical power systems

The project includes both:
- a fully constrained 3D assembly
- a detailed drawing with dimensions and parts list

---

## System Description

A planetary gear system consists of four main components:

- **Sun Gear** (central driving gear)
- **Planet Gears** (multiple gears rotating around the sun gear)
- **Planet Carrier** (holds and connects planet gears)
- **Ring Gear** (outer gear with internal teeth)

According to the drawing, the assembly includes:
- 1 × ring gear  
- 1 × planet carrier  
- 3 × planet gears  
- 1 × sun gear 

All components are designed using stainless steel (austenitic).

---

## How It Works

The planetary gear system distributes motion through multiple gear interactions:

- The **sun gear** drives the system (input)
- The **planet gears** rotate around the sun gear while also spinning on their own axes
- The **planet carrier** transmits motion from the planet gears
- The **ring gear** constrains and guides the motion

Depending on which component is fixed or driven, the system can:
- increase torque
- reduce speed
- change direction of rotation

This makes planetary gears highly efficient and compact.
